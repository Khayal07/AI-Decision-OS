# AI Decision OS

AI Decision OS is an intelligent decision-making platform that helps users make better choices using artificial intelligence.

Instead of being a simple comparison tool, AI Decision OS works as a personal AI decision assistant that can analyze different types of decisions — from choosing a laptop or university to career, finance, technology, travel, and everyday life choices.

Users can simply describe their decision, provide a few preferences, and the AI analyzes possible options, evaluates risks, compares alternatives, and generates a visual decision report with recommendations.

## Key Idea

Turn complex decisions into simple, data-driven, and personalized choices with AI.

## Main Features

- AI-powered decision analysis
- Multi-factor comparison and scoring
- Personalized recommendations
- Risk and benefit analysis
- Visual decision dashboards
- AI reasoning and explanations
- Decision history and preference learning

AI Decision OS aims to make decision-making faster, smarter, and more confident.
